“OBDA_main”is a scripting program used to perform numerical experiments under the OBDA mechanism.
“PricingAllocationRlue”is a function of payment and allocation rule under the OBDA mechanism.
“StaticPricingMethod_Gurobi”is a function of per-determined pricing rule by call Gurobi uner the OBDA mechanism.
“AdmissionRule”is a function of admission rule uner the OBDA mechanism.
“SurvivalRule”is a function of survival rule uner the OBDA mechanism.
“MaxSocialWelfare_MultiDemand_Gurobi”is a function to find the ex-post maxium social welfare by calling gurobi in the case with single-unit demand.
